Losing personal items such as IDs, wallets, gadgets, and other essentials is a common and frustrating issue on campus. Many items remain unclaimed due to the absence of an organized and reliable method for matching lost belongings with their rightful owners.

This project provides a **centralized, accessible, and efficient digital portal** where users can:
- Submit reports of lost or found items
- Browse and search existing posts
- Provide detailed descriptions, photos, dates, and locations
- Communicate with individuals who may have found their belongings

Instead of relying on social media posts or word of mouth, this system streamlines the entire process and ensures organized handling of lost and found cases.
